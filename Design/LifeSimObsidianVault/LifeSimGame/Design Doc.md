## 🧭 1. Core Vision

**High Concept:**

> A life simulation game that blends open-ended sandbox gameplay with a structured, mission-driven progression system.

**Player Fantasy:**

- “Start with nothing → build a life → unlock the world”
- Clear goals like a _game_, but freedom like a _sim_

**Design Pillars:**

1. **Guided Progression** – Always know what to do next
2. **Meaningful Unlocks** – Every goal expands gameplay
3. **Player Agency** – Missions guide, not restrict
4. **Systemic Depth** – Everything interacts (jobs, skills, relationships)

## 🧱 3. Game Structure

### 🎯 Dual-Layer Design (THIS is the magic)

#### Layer 1: Sandbox Simulation

- Needs system (Hunger, Energy, Fun, Social, etc.)
- Careers / jobs
- Skills (cooking, charisma, etc.)
- Relationships
- Housing & items

#### Layer 2: Mission System

- Structured objectives drive progression
- Unlock gated content
- Provide direction and pacing

---

## 🗺️ 4. Progression System

### 📈 “Life Path” Progression

Instead of one long story, the game is divided into:

### 🔓 **Chapters / Life Stages**

- Stage 1: “Starting Out” (tiny house, low job options)
- Stage 2: “Getting Stable”
- Stage 3: “Climbing Up”
- Stage 4: “Living Large”

Each stage:

- Unlocks new neighborhoods
- Unlocks careers
- Introduces new systems

---

## 📋 5. Mission System (THE KEY FEATURE)

### 🧩 Mission Types

#### 1. Core Missions (Progression)

- Required to advance stages
- Example:
    - “Get a job”
    - “Earn $500”
    - “Make 2 friends”

#### 2. Side Missions (Optional)

- Flavor + rewards
- Example:
    - “Throw a party”
    - “Cook 5 meals without burning them”

#### 3. System Unlock Missions

- Teach mechanics
- Example:
    - “Upgrade your kitchen”
    - “Learn level 2 cooking”

---

### 🧠 Mission Design Rules

- ❌ Never block player creativity
- ✅ Always suggest direction
- ✅ Reward with meaningful unlocks
- ✅ Stackable (multiple active at once)

---

## 🏠 6. Unlock System

### 🔐 Content is LOCKED behind progression

Unlike modern Sims, where everything is available:

|Unlock Type|Examples|
|---|---|
|Homes|Apartments → Houses → Luxury|
|Careers|Retail → Office → Specialized|
|Items|Cheap → Advanced → Luxury|
|Systems|Dating → Pets → Business ownership|

### 💬 Social System

## 🧑‍💼 8. Career System

- Entry → Mid → Advanced jobs
- Performance based on:
    - Needs
    - Skills
    - Mood

**Example:**

- Show up tired → bad performance
- High charisma → faster promotions

## 🧪 10. Game Modes

### 🎯 Story Mode (Bustin’ Out style)

- Full mission structure
- Progression gated

### 🏖️ Free Mode

- Everything unlocked
- No missions required

👉 This solves BOTH audiences

## ⚙️ 11. Systems Architecture (High-Level)

_(Speaking your language now)_

- **GameStateManager**
    - Tracks progression stage
- **MissionManager**
    - Handles active missions, completion, rewards
- **UnlockManager**
    - Controls gated content
- **SimController**
    - Needs + behaviors
- **EconomyManager**
    - Money flow
