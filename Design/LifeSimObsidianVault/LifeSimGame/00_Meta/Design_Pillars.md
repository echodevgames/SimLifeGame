## 1. Guided Progression
The player should always know what to do next.

## 2. Meaningful Unlocks
Every completed objective expands gameplay.

## 3. Player Agency
Players can ignore missions but are encouraged to follow them.

## 4. Systemic Depth
All systems interact:
- Needs affect work
- Work affects money
- Money affects progression
