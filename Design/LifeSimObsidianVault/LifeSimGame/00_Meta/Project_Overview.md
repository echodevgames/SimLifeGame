## Working Title
Life Sim: Structured Freedom

## High Concept
A life simulation game that combines open-ended sandbox gameplay with a structured, mission-driven progression system.

## Player Fantasy
Start with nothing → build a life → unlock the world

## Target Platform
- PC (Primary)
- Unity 6

## Genre
- Life Simulation
- Progression / Mission-Based Sandbox

## Inspiration
- The Sims: Bustin’ Out
- The Sims 4
- The Sims Medieval

## Unique Selling Point
A hybrid system that merges:
- Sandbox simulation (player freedom)
- Structured progression (clear goals and rewards)

## Design Goals
- Always give the player direction
- Make progression feel meaningful
- Avoid overwhelming the player
