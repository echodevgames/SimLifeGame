## Overview

The Needs System drives player behavior and decision-making.

---

## Core Needs

- Hunger
- Energy
- Hygiene
- Social
- Fun (optional expansion)

---

## Mechanics

- Needs decay over time
- Low needs negatively affect performance
- High needs provide bonuses

---

## Gameplay Impact

| Need | Effect |
|------|--------|
| Hunger | Reduces energy gain |
| Energy | Limits work efficiency |
| Hygiene | Affects social interactions |
| Social | Affects mood |

---

## Design Goals

- Create constant pressure
- Encourage varied gameplay
- Tie into all major systems
