## Loop Breakdown

1. Manage Needs
   - Hunger
   - Energy
   - Hygiene
   - Social

2. Maintain & Interact with Grow Setup
   - Check and manage production systems
   - Adjust layout or equipment
   - Ensure systems are running efficiently

3. Perform Daily Life Activities
   - Social interactions (build relationships)
   - Home management (cleaning, upgrading, organizing)
   - Personal progression

4. Generate Income
   - Production runs over time
   - Output is affected by setup quality and player interaction

5. Complete Missions
   - Progress through structured objectives
   - Unlock new systems and opportunities

6. Upgrade & Expand
   - Improve home and grow space
   - Purchase new equipment and items
   - Expand into larger areas (room → basement → property → warehouse)

7. Optimize & Scale
   - Improve efficiency
   - Refine layouts
   - Increase production output

8. Repeat with Increased Complexity
   - More systems
   - Larger scale
   - Greater rewards and challenges
