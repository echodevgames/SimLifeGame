## Structure

Progression is divided into stages based on both:

- Personal life development (home, relationships, needs)
- Grow operation expansion (space, equipment, efficiency)

Progression is primarily driven by completing missions and expanding your operation.

---

## Stages

### Stage 1 – Bare Minimum

- Small living space
- Basic grow setup (closet or small area)
- Limited resources
- Manual interaction required

---

### Stage 2 – Getting By

- Improved equipment
- Dedicated grow room or basement
- More consistent income
- Early optimization begins

---

### Stage 3 – Scaling Up

- Larger home or property
- Multiple grow areas (indoor + outdoor)
- Hiring first worker
- Layout and efficiency become important

---

### Stage 4 – Full Operation

- Large-scale setups (warehouse, farm, or multi-property)
- Advanced equipment and systems
- Automation and worker management
- High production output and optimization focus

---

## Progression Drivers

Players advance by:

- Completing missions
- Expanding grow setups
- Increasing production output
- Unlocking new spaces and systems

---

## Unlock Types

- [[Locations]] (homes, properties, new areas)
- Grow Spaces (closet → room → basement → warehouse)
- Equipment (basic → advanced → industrial)
- Systems (workers, automation, optimization tools)
- [[Items]] (furniture + grow equipment)

---

## Design Goals

- Tie progression directly to player actions and decisions
- Ensure visible growth in both home and operation
- Reward optimization and expansion
- Maintain a clear sense of forward momentum
