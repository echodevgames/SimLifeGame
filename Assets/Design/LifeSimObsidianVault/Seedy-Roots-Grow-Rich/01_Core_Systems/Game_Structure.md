## Overview

The game is structured as a hybrid between:

- Life simulation (player needs, relationships, home)
- Grow system (production, optimization, expansion)
- Mission-driven progression (structured objectives)

The core design blends these systems so that:
> The player’s home evolves into a fully functional production operation.

---

## Core Structure Model

### 🎯 Three-Layer Design

#### Layer 1: Life Simulation

- Needs system (Hunger, Energy, Hygiene, Social)
- Relationships and social progression
- Home building and furniture
- Daily routine and time management

---

#### Layer 2: Grow System (Core Twist)

- Production-based income system
- Fully integrated into the home
- Expands with player progression

Examples:
- Closet grow → room → basement → full property → warehouse/farm

---

#### Layer 3: Mission System

- Structured objectives drive progression
- Guides both life simulation and grow system development
- Unlocks new systems, spaces, and tools

---

## Game Modes

### 🎯 Story Mode

- Mission-driven progression
- Content locked behind objectives
- Bustin’ Out-style structured experience

---

### 🏖️ Free Mode

- All systems unlocked
- No progression restrictions
- Player-driven sandbox experience

---

## Player Flow

1. Start with limited space and basic setup
2. Manage needs and daily life
3. Begin small-scale grow operation
4. Receive and complete missions
5. Generate income from production
6. Upgrade home and expand grow space
7. Unlock new systems and opportunities
8. Scale operation and optimize efficiency
