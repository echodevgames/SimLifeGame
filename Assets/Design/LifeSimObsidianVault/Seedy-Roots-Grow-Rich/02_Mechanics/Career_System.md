## Overview

The Career System defines the player’s progression and identity within the game.

Instead of traditional employment, the player’s “career” is built through:

- Managing and expanding their grow operation
- Improving efficiency and production
- Scaling their home into a full production environment

---

## Core Concept

Your home is your workplace.

- Income is generated through production, not employment
- Progression comes from expansion and optimization
- The player never leaves their environment to “go to work”

---

## Structure

The player’s career evolves through stages of operational growth:

- Small-scale personal setup (closet / room)
- Dedicated grow space (room / basement)
- Multi-area operation (home + outdoor)
- Large-scale production (warehouse / farm)

---

## Progression Mechanics

Players advance their “career” by:

- Expanding grow space
- Improving equipment and systems
- Increasing production efficiency
- Hiring workers and scaling operations
- Completing missions tied to growth and expansion

---

## Performance Factors

Player success is influenced by:

- Needs (energy, hunger, hygiene, social)
- Efficiency of grow setup
- Time management and interaction
- System optimization decisions

---

## Integration with Systems

### 🌱 Grow System

- Core gameplay system that drives income and progression
- Directly defines the player’s career success

---

### 💰 Economy System

- Income from production fuels expansion
- Investment decisions impact long-term growth

---

### 📋 Mission System

- Missions define progression milestones
- Guide the player through career growth

---

### 💬 Social System

- Relationships unlock opportunities
- Enables worker hiring and progression paths

---

## Career Identity

The player’s “career” is not defined by a job title, but by:

- The scale of their operation
- The efficiency of their systems
- The progression of their environment

---

## Design Goals

- Replace traditional job systems with player-driven production
- Make progression feel personal and environment-based
- Integrate career progression with all major systems
- Reinforce the concept that the player builds their own success
