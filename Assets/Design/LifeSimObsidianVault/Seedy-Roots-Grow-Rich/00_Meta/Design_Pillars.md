## 1. Guided Progression

The player should always know what to do next.

- Missions provide clear short-term goals
- Progression is structured (Bustin’ Out style)
- The player is never left without direction

---

## 2. Meaningful Expansion

Every upgrade and unlock should visibly expand the player’s capabilities.

- Home evolves into a larger operation
- New spaces enable new systems
- Growth is both functional and visual

---

## 3. Integrated Systems

Life simulation and production systems are deeply connected.

- Needs affect productivity
- Home layout affects production
- Social progress unlocks new opportunities
- The player’s life and operation are inseparable

---

## 4. Player Agency

Players are guided, but not restricted.

- Missions suggest direction without forcing playstyle
- Multiple ways to optimize and expand
- Players choose how to balance life vs production

---

## 5. Systemic Depth

All systems interact to create emergent gameplay.

- Needs → affect efficiency
- Grow setup → affects income
- Income → enables expansion
- Expansion → increases system complexity

---

## 6. Optimization & Scaling

The player is rewarded for improving efficiency and planning ahead.

- Better layouts increase output
- Upgrades have measurable impact
- Scaling introduces new challenges and opportunities
