## Working Title
Seedy Roots: Grow Rich

---

## High Concept

A mission-driven life simulation game where your home doubles as a growing operation—combining everyday life management with production, optimization, and expansion.

---

## Player Fantasy

- Start dirt poor → build a life → turn your home into a thriving operation → get dirt rich
- Balance personal needs, relationships, and a growing production setup
- Transform a small personal grow into a large-scale operation

---

## Target Platform

- PC (Primary)
- Unity 6

---

## Genre

- Life Simulation
- Progression / Mission-Based Sandbox
- Systems-Driven Simulation

---

## Inspiration

- The Sims: Bustin’ Out (mission-driven structure)
- The Sims 4 (sandbox systems)
- Tycoon / management games (optimization + scaling)

---

## Unique Selling Point

A hybrid system where:

- Your **home is your business**
- Life simulation and production systems are fully integrated
- Progression is driven by both personal development and operational growth

---

## Design Goals

- Always give the player direction (mission-driven progression)
- Make progression feel meaningful through visible expansion
- Integrate life systems with production systems
- Reward optimization, planning, and scaling
- Maintain a balance between structure and player freedom

---

## Core Identity (IMPORTANT)

> “A life sim where your house slowly becomes a full production operation.”

This defines the entire experience and should be reflected in all systems.
