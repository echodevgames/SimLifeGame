## Overview

The Reward System provides the player with meaningful outcomes for completing missions and progressing through the game.

Rewards are designed to:

- Expand the player’s capabilities
- Improve production efficiency
- Unlock new gameplay opportunities

---

## Reward Types

### 💰 Money

- Earned from missions and production
- Used to purchase upgrades, equipment, and items
- Enables expansion and scaling

---

### 🧱 Space & Expansion

- Unlock new rooms, areas, or properties
- Increase available grow space
- Enable larger and more complex setups

---

### ⚙️ Equipment & Items

- New grow equipment (lights, tools, systems)
- Home furniture and upgrades
- Items that improve efficiency or comfort

---

### 🔓 System Unlocks

- Workers (automation support)
- Advanced grow mechanics
- Optimization tools
- New gameplay systems

---

### 📈 Progression Advancement

- Unlock next stage of gameplay
- Enable access to new missions
- Gate access to higher-tier content

---

## Reward Design Principles

- Rewards must have visible impact
- Rewards should directly improve gameplay capabilities
- Rewards should reinforce player decisions and strategies
- Rewards should support both life simulation and grow systems

---

## Role in Gameplay

Rewards act as the primary driver of progression by:

- Encouraging mission completion
- Enabling expansion of the player’s home and operation
- Increasing system complexity and player agency
