## Purpose

The Mission System provides structure, direction, and progression to the player.

It serves as the primary driver of gameplay by guiding both:

- Life simulation progression (relationships, home, needs)
- Grow system expansion (production, efficiency, scaling)

---

## Key Features

- Multiple active missions
- Clear, actionable objectives
- Visible progress tracking
- Meaningful rewards tied to progression
- Direct integration with all core systems

---

## Role in Gameplay

Missions act as the bridge between systems:

- Guide players on what to do next
- Introduce new mechanics gradually
- Unlock new spaces, tools, and systems
- Encourage both life simulation and production gameplay

## Example Mission Types

- Social:
  - Make 2 neighborhood friends
  - Host a social event

- Grow / Production:
  - Set up your first grow
  - Upgrade your grow space
  - Increase production output

- Expansion:
  - Expand into a new room or area
  - Hire your first worker

---

## Design Rules

- ❌ Never restrict sandbox play
- ✅ Always provide direction
- ✅ Reward meaningful progression
- ✅ Stackable (multiple active missions)
- ✅ Integrate both life sim and grow systems
