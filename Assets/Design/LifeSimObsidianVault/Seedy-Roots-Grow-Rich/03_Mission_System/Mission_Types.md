## Core Missions (Progression)

Core missions drive the main progression of the game.

They guide the player through both:
- Life simulation systems
- Grow system expansion

Examples:
- Set up your first grow
- Generate your first income
- Make 2 neighborhood friends
- Upgrade your grow setup
- Expand into a new room or space
- Hire your first worker

---

## Side Missions (Optional)

Optional missions provide additional rewards and encourage exploration of systems.

Examples:
- Maintain high production for a duration
- Optimize your grow layout
- Host a social event
- Improve home comfort
- Increase efficiency of your setup

---

## System Unlock Missions

These missions introduce and teach new mechanics.

Examples:
- Unlock advanced grow equipment
- Expand your grow capacity
- Improve production efficiency
- Unlock worker management
- Gain access to new locations

---

## Design Intent

Each mission type serves a purpose:

- Core Missions → Drive progression forward
- Side Missions → Encourage experimentation and optimization
- System Unlock Missions → Introduce new gameplay systems

All mission types should:
- Reinforce the core gameplay loop
- Connect life simulation and grow systems
- Provide meaningful and visible progression
