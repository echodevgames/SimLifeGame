## 🧭 1. Core Vision

**High Concept:**

> A mission-driven life simulation game where your home doubles as a growing operation—combining everyday life management with production, optimization, and expansion.

**Player Fantasy:**

- “Start dirt poor → build a life → turn your home into a thriving operation → get dirt rich”
- Balance personal needs, relationships, and a growing production setup

**Design Pillars:**

1. **Guided Progression** – Always have clear objectives (Bustin’ Out style)
2. **Integrated Systems** – Life sim and grow systems directly affect each other
3. **Meaningful Expansion** – Your home evolves into your business
4. **Player Agency** – Multiple ways to grow, optimize, and progress

---

## 🔁 2. Core Gameplay Loop

**Moment-to-moment loop:**

1. Manage needs (hunger, energy, hygiene, social)
2. Maintain and interact with your grow setup
3. Perform daily life activities (social, home management)
4. Generate income from production over time
5. Complete missions and objectives
6. Upgrade your home and expand your operation
7. Repeat with increased scale and complexity

---

## 🧱 3. Game Structure

### 🎯 Three-Layer Design

#### Layer 1: Life Simulation

- Needs system (Hunger, Energy, Hygiene, Social)
- Relationships and social interactions
- Home building and furniture
- Daily routines

#### Layer 2: Grow System (Core Twist)

- Production-based income system
- Fully integrated into the home
- Expands from small personal setups to large-scale operations

#### Layer 3: Mission System

- Structured objectives drive progression
- Connects life simulation and grow systems
- Unlocks new tools, spaces, and opportunities

---

## 🗺️ 4. Progression System

### 📈 Operation Growth Progression

Progression is tied to both personal life and grow operation expansion.

### 🔓 Stages

- Stage 1: “Bare Minimum”
  - Small living space
  - Basic grow setup (closet or small room)

- Stage 2: “Getting By”
  - Improved equipment
  - Dedicated grow room or basement

- Stage 3: “Scaling Up”
  - Larger home or property
  - Outdoor or multi-room setups
  - Hiring first worker

- Stage 4: “Full Operation”
  - Large-scale setups (warehouse/farm)
  - Advanced systems and optimization

Each stage:
- Expands available space
- Unlocks better equipment
- Introduces new systems

---

## 📋 5. Mission System

### 🧩 Mission Types

#### 1. Core Missions (Progression)

- Required to advance stages
- Examples:
  - Set up your first grow
  - Generate your first income
  - Make 2 neighborhood friends
  - Upgrade your grow setup
  - Expand into a new space
  - Hire your first worker

#### 2. Side Missions (Optional)

- Flavor + rewards
- Examples:
  - Maintain high output for a duration
  - Optimize your setup layout
  - Host a social event
  - Improve home comfort

#### 3. System Unlock Missions

- Teach mechanics
- Examples:
  - Unlock advanced equipment
  - Improve efficiency systems
  - Expand grow capacity

---

### 🧠 Mission Design Rules

- ❌ Never block player creativity
- ✅ Always provide direction
- ✅ Reward with meaningful unlocks
- ✅ Stackable (multiple active at once)

---

## ⚙️ 11. Systems Architecture (High-Level)

- **GameStateManager**
  - Tracks progression stage

- **MissionManager**
  - Handles active missions, completion, rewards

- **UnlockManager**
  - Controls gated content

- **SimController**
  - Handles needs and player state

- **GrowManager**
  - Handles production systems

- **EconomyManager**
  - Tracks income and spending
