## Overview

Locations represent the spaces where the player lives, builds, and expands their operation.

They define:

- Available space for grow setups
- Player living environment
- Progression scale and complexity

Locations are the primary driver of visual and gameplay expansion.

---

## Core Concept

The player progresses through increasingly larger and more complex spaces:

Closet → Room → Basement → House → Warehouse/Farm

Each new location:

- Expands available space
- Unlocks new systems
- Increases production potential

---

## Location Types

### 🏠 Living Spaces

Primary environments where the player lives and builds.

Examples:
- Small apartment
- Starter home
- Large house

---

### 🌱 Grow Spaces

Dedicated areas for production.

Examples:
- Closet grow
- Spare room
- Basement setup
- Outdoor garden
- Industrial space

---

### 🏭 Large-Scale Locations

Late-game environments for high production.

Examples:
- Warehouse
- Farm
- Multi-property setups

---

## Location Properties

Each location may define:

- Total available space
- Number of allowed grow setups
- Maximum equipment capacity
- Available systems (basic → advanced)
- Upgrade potential

---

## Integration with Systems

### 🌱 Grow System

- Determines how many setups can exist
- Controls scale of production
- Enables new production methods

---

### 💰 Economy System

- Larger locations require higher investment
- Higher cost = higher potential income

---

### 📋 Mission System

- Missions unlock new locations
- Some missions require expansion

---

### 🧱 Items System

- Determines where items can be placed
- Affects layout and efficiency

---

## Progression Role

Locations act as major progression milestones.

Each new location:

- Represents a new stage of gameplay
- Expands player capabilities
- Introduces new challenges and systems

---

## Design Goals

- Make expansion feel meaningful and visible
- Tie progression directly to space growth
- Encourage player planning and layout optimization
- Support increasing system complexity over time
