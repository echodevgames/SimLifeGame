## Overview

Items represent all placeable and usable objects in the game.

They serve both functional and cosmetic roles, and are central to:

- Grow system setup
- Home building and customization
- Player progression

---

## Item Categories

### 🌱 Grow Equipment

Items used in production systems.

Examples:
- Basic grow lights
- Advanced lighting systems
- Containers / pots
- Environmental equipment

---

### 🏠 Home & Furniture

Items used to build and customize living space.

Examples:
- Beds (affect energy recovery)
- Showers (affect hygiene)
- Seating / decor
- Storage

---

### ⚙️ Utility & Upgrade Items

Items that improve efficiency or unlock new capabilities.

Examples:
- Efficiency boosters
- Automation tools
- Advanced systems

---

## Item Properties

Each item may include:

- Cost
- Category
- Placement rules
- Effect on systems (needs, production, etc.)
- Upgrade tier

---

## Integration with Systems

### 🌱 Grow System

- Core components of production setups
- Directly impact output and efficiency

---

### 🧠 Needs System

- Furniture affects need recovery
- Example:
  - Bed → Energy
  - Shower → Hygiene

---

### 💰 Economy System

- Purchased using in-game currency
- Represent primary investment path

---

## Design Goals

- Make items feel meaningful and impactful
- Ensure every item has a clear gameplay purpose
- Support both life simulation and production systems
- Scale from basic to advanced tiers
