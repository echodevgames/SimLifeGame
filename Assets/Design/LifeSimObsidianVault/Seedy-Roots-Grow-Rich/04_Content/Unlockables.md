## Overview

The Unlockables system controls when content becomes available to the player.

It governs access to:

- Items
- Locations
- Systems
- Features

Unlockables are primarily driven by mission completion and progression stages.

---

## Core Concept

Players do not have access to all content from the start.

Instead, content is gradually unlocked through:

- Mission completion
- Progression milestones
- System-specific requirements

---

## Unlock Categories

### 🧱 Items

- Grow equipment
- Furniture
- Utility items

Unlocked through:
- Missions
- Progression stages

---

### 🏠 Locations

- New living spaces
- Expanded grow areas
- Large-scale production environments

Unlocked through:
- Major progression milestones
- Key mission completions

---

### ⚙️ Systems

- Workers
- Automation
- Advanced grow mechanics

Unlocked through:
- System unlock missions
- Progression stages

---

### 🔓 Features

- New gameplay mechanics
- UI elements
- Advanced interactions

---

## Unlock Triggers

Unlocks can be triggered by:

- Mission completion
- Reaching a progression stage
- Meeting system requirements (e.g., production thresholds)
- Achieving social milestones

---

## Integration with Systems

### 📋 Mission System

- Primary driver of unlocks
- Rewards often include new content access

---

### 🗺️ Progression System

- Defines what can be unlocked at each stage
- Prevents early access to advanced systems

---

### 🌱 Grow System

- Unlocks new equipment and production capabilities
- Expands operational complexity

---

### 💬 Social System

- Unlocks new opportunities, contacts, or systems
- Enables progression through relationships

---

## Player Experience

Unlockables should:

- Provide clear goals and rewards
- Create anticipation for new content
- Reinforce progression and growth
- Feel meaningful and impactful

---

## Design Goals

- Control progression pacing
- Prevent overwhelming the player early
- Ensure content is introduced at the right time
- Tie all unlocks to player actions and achievements
